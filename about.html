About Us

Why Choose Juniper Safety

Located in Grand Haven, MI

Our instructors are the best in what they do. They are certified through the American Heart Association or AHA. They have trained and issued same day certifications to hundreds of students in the healthcare and non healthcare field for many years.

We conduct weekly CPR classes at our site in Grand Haven, MI. We offer regular First Aid, CPR, AED, and BLS classes on Tuesday evenings. Scheduling a class is convenient with our online booking system. Can't find a class that works for you? Call (616) 202-6496.

Do you have a group of people to train to meet OSHA requirements or you just prefer to have the training done at your location? Do not worry, we offer onsite CPR training and we will come to your place of business to run the training.

We provide CPR, First Aid, AED and BLS training in surrounding areas (Grand Haven, Grand Rapids, Holland, Muskegon, West Olive, Coopersville, Allendale, Nunica, Norton Shores, Fruitport, Ferrysburg, Spring Lake, Walker, Sparta, and more!)

We understand that training can be very intimidating and oftentimes an anxiety provoking event so we make sure that we conduct our classes in a stress free and easy to understand manner. Our goal is to help you learn CPR and First Aid so you can apply what you learn in our classroom to any emergency situation you may encounter later in life.
